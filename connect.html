Community Involvement

Garden Donation Program

We partner with local schools, community gardens, and nonprofits to donate plants, seeds, and gardening supplies. Our goal is to help create green spaces that bring people together and promote sustainability. If you know of a community project in need, reach out to us—we’d love to help!

Seasonal Gardening Workshops

We host free, hands-on gardening workshops throughout the year to educate and inspire our community. From beginner-friendly planting guides to eco-friendly gardening techniques, our workshops are open to all ages and experience levels. Join us for our next event and grow your gardening skills with us!

Collaborations and Events

Bloom Valley Nursery loves partnering with local businesses, organizations, and gardening enthusiasts to bring exciting events and collaborations to life! Whether you’re interested in hosting a workshop, planning a community gardening event, or collaborating on a special project, we’d love to hear from you. Want to collaborate with us? Reach out to our team or visit us in-store to discuss how we can work together to create something special for our community!
